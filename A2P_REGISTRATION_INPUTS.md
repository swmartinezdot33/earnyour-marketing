## Use Case Description
*(Required: 40-4096 characters)*

```
This campaign sends marketing and transactional SMS messages to customers who have provided their phone number and explicitly opted in to receive text messages from EarnYour Marketing. Messages include marketing communications about our digital marketing services (SEO, Google Ads, Facebook Ads, websites, CRM automation, and custom software solutions), service updates, appointment confirmations and reminders for consultations and audit reviews, follow-up messages after form submissions, and other business communications. All recipients have provided explicit consent to receive SMS messages from EarnYour Marketing by checking an opt-in checkbox on our website forms and agreeing to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Message frequency varies based on customer interactions and service requests. Customers can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.
```
