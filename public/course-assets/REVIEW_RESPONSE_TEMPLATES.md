## üìù WRITING TIPS FOR RESPONSES

### ‚úÖ DO:
- Address the reviewer by name (builds rapport)
- Be specific about what they mentioned (shows you read it)
- Keep responses short (2-3 sentences max)
- Be professional but warm
- Include a call-to-action (book again, call, refer)
- Respond to negative reviews quickly
- Thank them for their feedback
- Offer to resolve issues

### ‚ùå DON'T:
- Use generic copy-paste responses (customers can tell)
- Get defensive on bad reviews
- Make excuses
- Write long paragraphs
- Use too many emojis
- Ignore 1 or 2-star reviews
- Respond when emotional
- Ask them to delete the review

## üéØ RESPONSE STRATEGY BY TYPE

### For 5-Star Reviews:
- **Goal**: Build brand loyalty and encourage referrals
- **Tone**: Grateful and enthusiastic
- **Action**: Invite them to refer friends

### For 4-Star Reviews:
- **Goal**: Find out what could be improved
- **Tone**: Appreciative and curious
- **Action**: Ask for feedback to earn 5 stars

### For 3-Star Reviews:
- **Goal**: Understand the issue and improve
- **Tone**: Professional and responsive
- **Action**: Invite dialogue to solve problem

### For 2-Star Reviews:
- **Goal**: Damage control and resolution
- **Tone**: Apologetic and solution-focused
- **Action**: Direct call/email to resolve

### For 1-Star Reviews:
- **Goal**: Turn customer around or prevent reputation damage
- **Tone**: Accountable and urgent
- **Action**: Personal outreach from owner/manager
