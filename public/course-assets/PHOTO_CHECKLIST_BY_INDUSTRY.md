## ðŸ’¡ PHOTO TIPS FOR MAXIMUM IMPACT

### Lighting:
- [ ] Shoot during daytime when possible
- [ ] Natural light is best
- [ ] Avoid harsh shadows
- [ ] Avoid backlighting (unless intentional)
- [ ] Use even lighting

### Composition:
- [ ] Rule of thirds (subject not centered)
- [ ] Close enough to see details
- [ ] Far enough to see context
- [ ] Clean background
- [ ] Professional framing

### Subjects:
- [ ] Real work/people (not stock photos)
- [ ] Clear focus on main subject
- [ ] Professional appearance
- [ ] Show action when possible
- [ ] Include people when you can

### Before & After:
- [ ] Same angle for both photos
- [ ] Same lighting conditions
- [ ] Clear transformation visible
- [ ] Professional presentation
- [ ] Customer consent obtained

## ðŸ“¸ PHOTO SOURCES

### Taking Your Own:
- Use smartphone camera (modern phones are excellent)
- Shoot in good light (daytime preferred)
- Multiple angles of same subject
- Tripod helps for consistent framing

### Professional Photographer:
- Hire for initial batch (20-30 photos)
- Great investment ($200-500)
- Professional quality
- High resolution
- Can do before/after shoots
- Great for team/office photos

### Hybrid Approach:
- Professional: Logo, cover, team, office
- DIY: Ongoing work/project photos
- Best of both: Quality + quantity
