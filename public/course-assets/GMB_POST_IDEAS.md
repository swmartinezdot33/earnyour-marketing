## ğŸ“‹ HOW TO USE THIS LIST

### Weekly Schedule Template:
- **Monday**: Educational tip (#11-20)
- **Wednesday**: Offer/Promotion (#1-10)
- **Friday**: Social proof or call-to-action (#21-27, #41-45)

### Customization Tips:
1. Replace [Your Business], [service], [number], [date] with your info
2. Add your actual phone number/booking link
3. Adjust to match your business type
4. Keep tone professional but friendly
5. Use emojis sparingly (1-2 per post max)

### Best Practices:
- Post 2-3 times per week
- Post at same times each week
- Respond to all comments same day
- Rotate between offer, education, and social proof
- Update dates and offers as needed

### Post Length:
- Keep under 150 characters for max reach
- Include clear call-to-action
- Use natural language (not salesy)
- Mention specific services/benefits
