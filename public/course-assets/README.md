### Post Ideas:

### Content Calendar:

### Review Responses:

- Use the templates as-is or customize
- Be specific about what they mentioned
- Always include next step (book again, refer, etc.)

- Send email 24 hours after service
- Send SMS 3-7 days later if no review
- Use direct review link (not GMB profile link)
