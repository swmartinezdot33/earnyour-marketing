## üìû WHEN TO SEND

### Email Timing:
- **Best Day**: Next business day after service completion
- **Best Time**: 10am - 2pm or 6pm - 8pm
- **Frequency**: Send once, then follow up with SMS

### SMS Timing:
- **Best Day**: 2-3 days after email (if no action yet)
- **Best Time**: 7pm - 9pm (evening when people check phones)
- **Frequency**: One SMS, max two if high-value

### Sequence Recommendation:
1. Email day after service (warm follow-up)
2. SMS 3 days later if no review yet (reminder)
3. Wait 1 week before contacting again

## üéØ GETTING YOUR GOOGLE REVIEW LINK

### How to Create Your Direct Review Link:
1. Go to Google My Business
2. On the Overview page, click "Customers" 
3. Under "Send review requests," copy your link
4. This is your direct review link - use it in emails/SMS

**It looks like:**
```
https://search.google.com/local/writereview?placeid=[UNIQUE_ID]
```

## üí° BEST PRACTICES

### ‚úÖ DO:
- Send within 24-48 hours of service completion
- Personalize with customer name
- Keep it short (email <100 words, SMS 1 message)
- Use conversational tone
- Be genuine and authentic
- Include your business name
- Use direct review links
- Follow up if no response in 3 days
- Thank them even if they don't review

### ‚ùå DON'T:
- Send too many emails/SMS
- Sound desperate or pushy
- Ask them to edit bad reviews
- Only ask people you're unsure about
- Use fake review services
- Ask friends/family only
- Send at midnight
- Use overly formal language
- Make it complicated to review

## üìà INCENTIVE IDEAS (Use Strategically)

**These work but must be appropriate:**
- $5-10 off next service
- Free add-on service
- Entry into monthly raffle
- Free upgrade or consultation
- Loyalty points

**Better incentive phrasing:**
"Leave a review and get [reward] on your next visit" vs. "Review us and get paid"

---

## üö® RED FLAGS TO AVOID

‚ùå **Never:**
- Offer money for positive reviews only
- Penalize negative reviews
- Ask customers to delete bad reviews
- Buy fake reviews
- Share customers' personal reviews without permission
- Contact customers repeatedly after no response

‚úÖ **Instead:**
- Ask all customers to review
- Incentivize the act of reviewing (not the star rating)
- Respond professionally to all reviews
- Use reviews to improve your service
