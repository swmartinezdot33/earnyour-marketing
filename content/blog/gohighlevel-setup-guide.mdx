## The Essential GHL Setup

### Step 1: Import Your Contacts

Start by importing:
- Past customers (from spreadsheets, old CRM)
- Current leads (from forms, phone calls)
- Email subscribers

**Pro Tip**: Clean your data first. Remove duplicates, fix phone numbers.

### Step 2: Set Up Your Pipelines

Create pipelines for your sales process:

**Example for HVAC:**
1. New Lead
2. Estimate Scheduled
3. Estimate Completed
4. Proposal Sent
5. Closed Won
6. Closed Lost

Move contacts through stages as they progress.

### Step 3: Automate Review Requests

**The Setup:**
1. Create a "Job Complete" tag
2. Trigger: When tag is added
3. Action: Send SMS with review link 1 hour later
4. Follow-up: Email 24 hours later if no review

**Result**: 80%+ review conversion rate (vs. 5% manual asking)

### Step 4: Missed Call Text Back

**The Problem**: You miss a call, they call your competitor instead.

**The Solution**: 
1. GHL detects missed call
2. Automatically sends SMS: "Hi, we missed your call. How can we help?"
3. 80% of people respond

**Result**: Recover 30-40% of missed calls

### Step 5: Lead Nurture Sequences

Not every lead is ready to buy immediately. Set up nurture sequences:

**For Service Businesses:**
- Day 1: Welcome email with service overview
- Day 3: Customer testimonials
- Day 7: Special offer or discount
- Day 14: "Still interested?" check-in

**For Professional Services:**
- Educational content series
- Case studies
- Free resources
- Consultation reminders
